Our Story

Who We Are

A dedicated construction company built on trust, skill, and results

GL Construction Builders is a growing construction company focused on delivering solid workmanship and dependable service. Led by Luis Illachuma, our company is built on dedication, responsibility, and attention to detail.

With hands-on field expertise, we specialize in structural work, interior finishing, and complete remodeling projects. We understand that every property is an investment, and our goal is to improve its functionality, appearance, and long-term value.

We proudly serve homeowners and property managers by providing practical, efficient, and professional construction solutions tailored to each project's needs.

Our Mission

To provide quality construction and remodeling services with integrity, precision, and efficiency — ensuring every project is completed safely and professionally.

Our Vision

To become a trusted construction company known for strong craftsmanship, reliability, and consistent results in residential improvement projects.
